Today sweatshirt became one of the main attributes of the wardrobe of all young people. The story of this thing directly relates to marginalized individuals and rappers of the eighties and nineties of the twentieth century. But actually sweatshirt roots extend far to the beginning of the twentieth century.

One day, son of Ben Russell, who was the owner of a textile factory in Alabama, complained to his father that it is uncomfortable to play football in a sweater made of wool. In fact, people were playing in sweaters in order to give no time for muscles to cool down. But it was incredibly uncomfortable, so caring father made his son a sweater of thick cotton.

This way Ben's company began to produce these hoodies for son's football team. Later, a colleague of Russell came up with the idea for the name of this thing - sweatshirt. So the beginning of sweatshirts was laid.

After a while, they complement with these sweaters not only football, but other sport teams. Russell later created a whole production line for sweatshirts called Russel Athletic, which is still in effect. Nowadays in manufacturing these sweatshirts dry fit technology is used. Later The Champion company began to produce cotton sweatshirts with logos of different football teams, making them an integral part of a team and fans subculture.

In the early eighties sweatshirts migrated from the wardrobe of professional athletes in those of ordinary people who wanted to lead a healthy lifestyle.

Classic sweatshirt models are made of cotton and fleece. as a last resort, they are made of polyester. On the sleeves and bottom of the sweater is gum, which reminds about the sport purpose of sweatshirt.

Long time has passed since sweatshirts were only the things for the sport. Today, you can wear it as you want to wear. Ideally you should wear sweatshirts with classic Nike sneakers high or low Vans. In some cases, it can be worn over a light-colored shirt. Velvet trousers or dark jeans are the best choice to wear with sweatshirts. And any football club bombers jackets should go, too.

Today all the fashion brands offer different variations of sweatshirts. From a purely sporting, to the incredibly feminine model (this refers to girls who love the comfort and elegance). The abundance of accessories, fashion prints and inscriptions make each sweatshirt a unique one.
