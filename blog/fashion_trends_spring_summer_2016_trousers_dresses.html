Fashion trends spring-summer 2016. Trousers and dresses.

Minimalism is the trend not for the first season. Excesses, pretentiousness and pomposity are no longer held in high esteem with the well-known brands, but simple and concise things are most preferred for international designers. There are no strict canons in this current. There is no decoration in clothes, cut is as simple as possible, and the materials are of high quality. However, at the fashion week other images were presented this year. Lace, jewelry from rhinestones, embroidery patterns, ribbons of fringe, contrasting colors, floral motifs. Famous designers have shown that in the current fashion should not be monotony and routine, "gray" and stamped images.

Trousers

For women relevant in this season will be the almost all trousers, from classic options to various capris, bermudas or the palazzo. One condition is that they must delicately emphasize the figure, not delivering at the same time a feeling of tightness. Bright and rich colors of trousers will be the most fashionable, but the red is on the first place. By tailoring many models of trousers are like jeans, blends harmoniously with embroidery or sequins.

Fashion trends this summer will be on the side of the brave girls, as the shortened tops will be a current. Moreover, women at any age and of any build can wear. Overestimated waist - is the general rule when low is more relevant for young girls.

Dresses

It is important to make the right choice of dress, because it is possible to be fashionable and attractive, in spite of the weather and time of year. In the coming season, designers promise a diversity of everyday dresses and sundresses - from romantic summer to strict office ones.

For shopping or city walks stylists advise to take the linen and cotton dresses, sundresses from denim material and clothes of safari style. Sports items can diversify knitted models. The most fashionable dresses will be of straight silhouette, simple cut, of calf-length, the mini or floor-length dresses. Many designers are using drapery to emphasize certain parts of the body, these dresses will look very expressive. Drapery emphasize the waist and hips, add romance and elegancy to the image. In 2016 prints with geometric pictures, floral patterns and stripes will become fashionable. Dresses of pastel colors will be decorated with cartoon characters and animal pictures. Many international designers such as Tommy Hilfiger, Michael Kors, Louis Vuitton, Prada, Antonio Marras emphasize the woman's waist, highlighting it using corsetry items, belts.

Paris Fashion Week is remarkable because of a combination of shoes and mini dresses. Summer heat created ballerina-models, and the usual small denim suit is equipped with the Nike socks and yellow boots. Stylists point out that in the coming season a combination of incongruous elements and airy cut dresses will be stylish. The models of different styles will be fashionable: with a collar, in the style of shirts, coats, strict dresses and dresses with wide skirts-bells. Colors can also be quite different. Stylists are convinced that the main thing in the form of a woman is that it should be unforgettable and unique.
