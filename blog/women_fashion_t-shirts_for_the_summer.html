How to choose T-shirts for the road

There are several simple rules on how to choose women's shirts for a long trip:

  • Be sure to take at least one white T-shirts or strait tailoring. It can be successfully combined with both jeans and a business pencil skirt.
  • A T-shirt of bright colors with decorative elements is always needed in order to have lunch or dinner with the company. These women's t-shirts today are not difficult to acquire. They are decorated with embroidery or applique.
  • You should take a classic T-shirt of pastel colors. It can be put on with both trousers and a skirt. The accessory can be a light small silk handkerchief or a string of bright beads. If you wear a shirt with a jacket, you can decorate it with a brooch or a flower.

If you want to buy women's t-shirts with a picture on it, then it is necessary to approach the selection very seriously.

Why the T-shirts with a large picture on them are dangerous for women

While choosing a T-shirt with a picture it is necessary to take into account the woman's age and her body type. Large bright picture visually makes the figure plumper. An incorrectly chosen picture can distort shapes.

For example, a picture of something big on chest, let it be flowers, makes the chest visually volume. It is suitable for young skinny girls, and it will be strangely looking at women of mature years.

If you decide to buy women's T-shirts with a picture, let it be small flowers, abstraction, but not figures of animals or people's faces. This subject should be left to the youth and adolescents.

When buying it is important to pay attention to the knitwear. Dense knitted T-shirts are suitable for denim and sport wear. For a business suit it will be successful to choose dense, but thin soft and slightly silky knitwear.

There are a lot of T-shirts on store shelves, we just have to think, with what we plan it to wear. We must remember that the right wardrobe is not the huge number of T-shirts but several well-chosen ones.
