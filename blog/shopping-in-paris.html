Shopping in Paris

Going to Paris, every fashionista dreams of bringing back home as many interesting and original things as possible from this beautiful city which can cause envy friends. Thus, it is important to understand where you can find places to fill up your wardrobe with splendid outfits.

Pleasant shopping on the Champs Elysees

If you have planned the trip to the French capital, then you should visit the Champs Elysees. Here you will be able to buy products of famous brands such as Adidas, Mango, Reebok and H&M. In order to please the children, you should drop in the Disney store, where you can buy beautiful dresses with your favorite characters and a variety of kids toys. When you get here you feel like you are in a fairy tale.

Actually, there are not only few boutiques on the Champs Elysees. But special attention deserves Louis Vuitton, the queue for handbags of the brand is the biggest of all. Their prices are up to two thousand dollars, but due to the fact that they can complement any image by making it even more attractive and original, their popularity never fades. The boutique Gallery Lafayette

Visiting Lafayette, you will see that here the best things for the whole family are collected. Everyone will be able to find something interesting and attractive. Immediately after entering the first building you will captivate the aroma of various perfumes, among which even the most demanding person will be able to choose the one that is ideal for him. Downstairs, you will see an abundance of women's shoes for all seasons. After rising to the upper floors, you can buy exquisite fur and leather products, as well as luxurious outfits for beautiful ladies. On the top floor of the building there are children's stores where you can buy clothes, shoes and toys for kids. But you should note that the prices are higher than in common stores of Paris.

The second sector of Lafayette is dedicated entirely to men. Here you can find clothes, shoes and accessories of famous brands. Furthermore, on the ground floor a variety of delicacies and expensive wines can be bought. In the third sector, which is located across the street from the first two, you can find everything you need for the home: furniture, accessories, kitchenware, bedding, and even bathrobes.

Going on shopping in Paris, you should definitely look at the Champs Elysees and Lafayette to please yourself and your family with the beautiful outfits and other important things.
